About This Project

Welcome to Berojgar's Notes. This platform was created to simplify Computer Science for NEB Class 11 students.

The goal is to move away from rote memorization and focus on understanding the logic behind the technology. Whether it's binary conversions or web development basics, everything here is designed to be clear and practical.

Our Mission

  • Provide high-quality, free educational resources.
  • Break down complex CS concepts into simple steps.
  • Help students prepare for exams while gaining real-world skills.
